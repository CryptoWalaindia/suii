Wallets, Keys & Self-Custody  —  12 TERMS

01
COLD WALLET
A cold wallet is a Bitcoin or crypto wallet setup that keeps private keys offline or awayfrom regular internet exposure. It is often used for long-term storage.
→
02
CUSTODIAL WALLET
A custodial wallet is a wallet where a third party, such as a crypto exchange, controlsthe private keys for you. You access your funds through an account, but the companymanages the keys.
→
03
HARDWARE WALLET
A hardware wallet is a physical device designed to protect private keys and sign Bitcointransactions. It helps keep keys away from regular internet-connected devices.
→
04
HOT WALLET
A hot wallet is a Bitcoin or crypto wallet connected to the internet. It is useful for quickaccess and regular transactions, but it may carry more online security risk than coldstorage.
→
05
MULTISIG WALLET
A multisig wallet is a Bitcoin wallet that requires multiple keys to approve a transaction.Instead of one private key controlling the wallet, a multisig setup needs more than oneapproval.
→
06
NON-CUSTODIAL WALLET
A non-custodial wallet is a wallet where you control your own private keys or seedphrase. This gives you direct control over your Bitcoin, but it also means you areresponsible for keeping your backup

07
PASSPHRASE

08
PRIVATE KEY
A private key is a secret digital key that proves you have the right to spend Bitcoin froma wallet. It must never be shared. If someone gets your private key, they may be able tomove your Bitcoin.
→
09
PUBLIC KEY
A public key is a cryptographic key connected to a private key. It helps receive Bitcoinand verify digital signatures. Unlike a private key, a public key is not meant to be secretin the same way.

10
RECOVERY PHRASE

11
SEED PHRASE
A seed phrase is a group of words that works as a backup for a Bitcoin or crypto wallet.If your wallet app, phone, or device is lost, the seed phrase can help restore access tothe wallet.
→
12
SELF-CUSTODY
Self-custody means you control your own Bitcoin or crypto keys instead of relying on anexchange or company to hold them for you. It gives more control, but it also gives youmore responsibility.
